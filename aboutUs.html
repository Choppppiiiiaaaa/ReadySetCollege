Our Story

Our site was made in efforts to help those who do not have access to information on college and how to apply. We are encouraging other students to pursue higher education, and prepare for their college education. Some are made out to believe that college is not achievable due to it being extremely expensive, but with the help from our site, it will inform students on how to make college possible for themselves. Thanks to Girls Who Code, four girls were brought together from around the Bay Area and came together to create College Insider. Girls Who Code is a free program that exposes rising junior and senior girls into Computer Science. This program helps break the gender gap within the STEM field, and encourages women to not undermine their abilities. It teaches women that it is okay to pursue a career that involves critical thinking and logic.
